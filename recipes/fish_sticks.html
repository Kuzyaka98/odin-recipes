Fish Sticks

Everbody loved this as kids and as adults, they are easy to make and easily devoured!

Ingredients

  • White Fish
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Seasoning
  • Egg, water and all purpose flour

Steps

  1. Overheat the oven
  2. Season and dip the fish in flour and egg to coat
  3. Bake the fish
  4. Add the sauce to your liking
